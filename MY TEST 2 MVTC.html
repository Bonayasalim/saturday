About Us

Madogo Vocational Training Centre is committed to providing top-notch vocational training in various fields. Our goal is to empower the youth with skills that lead to employment and self-reliance.

Courses We Offer

  • Electrical and Electronic Engineering: Learn how to design, install, and maintain electrical systems.
  • Fashion Design: Develop skills in fashion design, tailoring, and garment construction.
  • Hairdressing: Master the art of hairdressing, styling, and beauty techniques.
  • ICT Courses: Get trained in computer and IT skills for the modern workplace.

Career Opportunities

Explore career opportunities and internships with Madogo Vocational Training Centre. We collaborate with industries to offer practical exposure for our students.
